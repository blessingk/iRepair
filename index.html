About Keltech Repairs

Keltech Repairs is a company that was established in 2010 with the aim of offering a cost effective yet competitive quality service in repairing of All Apple devices (iPod, iPad, MacBook, iMac and iPhone). We specialize in chip level repairs, which has proved to be our greatest strength as most of our competitors only do logic board replacements. Personalised attention and convenience, all done at the minimal turn around time of 24 hours.
